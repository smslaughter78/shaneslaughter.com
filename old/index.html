About

Hospitality roots. Production scale. Builder's mindset.

My career started behind the scenes in hotels, where I learned “The Spirit to Serve” from the Marriott family of properties — anticipate needs, act with integrity, embrace change. Those values carried me through two decades in event technology and production, from running AV at one of Marriott's largest properties to directing a 40-person production organization and a nine-figure P&L at Encore Global.

As VP of Production Services at PRA, I led a national team of Executive Producers delivering complex corporate events for some of the world's most recognizable brands — sharpening how I think about standards, margins, and the craft of making enormous logistical efforts look effortless.

Today I'm doing the most meaningful work of my career: running fleet operations for Learning Undefeated, a STEM-education nonprofit that brings mobile laboratory experiences directly to under-resourced schools. I designed and built the technology systems inside our fleet of mobile labs — nine new builds and five full renovations and counting — and I own the operation that keeps them rolling, from routing and maintenance to the scheduling and dispatch systems behind the scenes.

What I'm focused on now

  • Designing and building the technology systems inside new mobile laboratories — 9 new builds and 5 renovations delivered
  • Scaling mobile STEM lab operations across Maryland, Texas, and the Gulf region
  • Custom operations tooling — scheduling, live fleet tracking, and driver systems
  • Doing more with less: stretching every mile and every dollar toward student reach
